Listed below is a collection of movies which Guy Ritchie has directed and/or written.

Lock, Stock, and Two Smoking Barrels

A botched card game in London triggers four friends, thugs, weed-growers, hard gangsters, loan sharks and debt collectors to collide with each other in a series of unexpected events, all for the sake of weed, cash and two antique shotguns.

Snatch

Two unlicensed boxing promoters get pulled into the world of match fixing. Things get complicated when they then try to convince a gypsy fighter not only to fight for them, but to throw a fight. Whilst all this is going on, a huge diamond heist takes place, and a fistful of motley characters enter the story. Things go from bad to worse as it all becomes about the money, the guns, and the damned dog!

Star (Short)

In this fast-paced and thrilling short, The Driver now carries an arrogant rock star who is visiting a major city (not Pittsburgh as earlier believed). Played by Madonna, this title character wants to get away from her bodyguards in the Driver's BMW. He soon gets tired of her and decides to have a bit of fun.

RockNRolla

In London, a real-estate scam puts millions of pounds up for grabs, attracting some of the city's scrappiest tough guys and its more established underworld types, all of whom are looking to get rich quick. While the city's seasoned criminals vie for the cash, an unexpected player -- a drugged out rock 'n' roller presumed to be dead but very much alive -- has a multi-million dollar prize fall into his hands.

Sherlock Holmes

When lord Blackwood mysteriously returns from the grave and resumes his killing spree, Holmes must take up the hunt. Contending with his partner's new fiancée and the dimwitted head of Scotland Yard, the dauntless detective must unravel the clues that will lead him into a twisted web of murder, deceit, and black magic - and the deadly embrace of temptress Irene Adler.

Sherlock Holmes:
A Game of Shadows

Sherlock Holmes and his longtime trusted associate, Doctor Watson, are back at it again. This time they take on their arch-nemesis, Professor Moriarty, with the help of Holmes' older brother Mycroft Holmes and a mysterious gypsy named Simza.

The Man from U.N.C.L.E

Three spies unexpectedly find themselves working together in a joint mission to stop a private criminal organization from using scientific expertise to construct their own nuclear bomb. Through clenched teeth and stylish poise, all three must find a way to cooperate for the sake of world peace, even as they each pursue their own agendas.

King Arthur: Legend of the Sword

The young Arthur runs the back passages of Londonium with his crew, not knowing his royal lineage until he grabs Excalibur. Instantly confronted by the sword's influence, Arthur is forced to make up his mind. He joins the rebellion and a shadowy young woman named Guinevere, he must learn to understand the magic weapon, deal with his demons and unite the people to defeat the dictator Vortigern, the man who murdered his parents and stole his crown to become king.
