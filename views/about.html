About

There's no doubt that Seattle is a beautiful city, and it's parks are no exception! Here you will find an easy to use directory of the best parks in the city, categorized by the classics - long time favorites and must see locations, and the hidden gems - which are tiny, little known parks scattered throughout the city that you may not even be aware of as a Seattle native! Additionally, be sure to check out the Secret Beaches section if you're down for an adventure.
